Matcha Latte

A refreshing and vibrant iced matcha latte to brighten your day.

save this recipe?

ingredients

  • 1 teaspoon (2g) high-quality matcha powder
  • 2-3 tablespoons hot water (around 175°F / 80°C - just below boiling)
  • 1 cup (240ml) cold milk of your choice (dairy, oat, almond, soy, etc.)
  • A handful of ice cubes
  • Optional: 1-2 teaspoons sweetener (simple syrup, agave, honey), to taste

tools

  • Small bowl or matcha bowl (chawan)
  • Bamboo whisk (chasen) OR a small regular whisk/frother
  • Tall serving glass
  • Spoon or straw for stirring

instructions

  1. Sift the Matcha: Place the matcha powder into your small bowl. Sifting prevents clumps and helps create a smooth latte.
  2. Whisk with Water: Add the hot water to the matcha powder. Using your bamboo whisk (or small regular whisk/frother), whisk briskly in a "W" or "M" motion until the matcha is fully dissolved and a light foam forms on top. This is your concentrated "matcha shot."
  3. Prepare Your Glass: Fill your tall serving glass generously with ice cubes.
  4. Add Milk & Sweetener: Pour the cold milk over the ice. If using sweetener, add it now and stir well to combine with the milk.
  5. Combine & Serve: Slowly pour your prepared matcha shot directly over the milk and ice. Watch as the vibrant green cascades into the white, creating a beautiful layered effect.
  6. Stir & Enjoy! Stir gently with a spoon or straw until the matcha is fully incorporated into the milk. Sip and enjoy your refreshing iced matcha latte!
